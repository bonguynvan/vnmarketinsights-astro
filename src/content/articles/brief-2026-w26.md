# Vietnam Market Brief — Week 26, 2026

## Overview

Vietnam's economy showed mixed momentum this week, with exports growing 21% year-on-year but imports accelerating faster at 33.1%, resulting in a $16.8 billion trade deficit in the first half of 2026. On a positive note, Vietnamese enterprises are shifting toward high-technology sectors, with over one-third of export value now coming from electronics and advanced products, while regulators approved infrastructure investments including the Can Gio-Vung Tau sea bridge project. However, authorities intensified crackdowns on fraud schemes—including a record 3.3 trillion VND holiday scam and illegal invoice trafficking—while also addressing banking security concerns and implementing new e-commerce regulations set for July 2026.
