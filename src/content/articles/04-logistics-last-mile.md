# Logistics & Last-mile Delivery Vietnam

## Market Overview

Vietnam's logistics market reached $55-60 billion in 2024, representing approximately 14-16% of GDP. The sector is characterized by rapid e-commerce-driven growth, infrastructure development, and increasing foreign investment.

## Market Structure

### By Service Type
- **Freight transport**: 65-70% of market
- **Warehousing**: 15-18% of market
- **Last-mile delivery**: 12-15% of market
- **Value-added services**: 3-5% of market

### By Transport Mode
- **Road**: 77% of freight volume
- **Waterway**: 17% of freight volume
- **Rail**: 3% of freight volume
- **Air**: 0.1% volume (high value goods)

## Key Players

### Domestic Logistics Companies

**Vietnam Post (VNP)**
- State-owned, widest coverage network
- 13,000+ post offices nationwide
- Universal service obligation (remote areas)
- Modernizing with e-commerce focus

**Giao Hang Nhanh (GHN)**
- Private courier, e-commerce specialized
- 2,000+ employees, 63 provinces covered
- Same-day delivery in major cities
- GHN Express: International services

**Giao Hang Tiet Kiem (GHTK)**
- Cost-efficient model
- Strong in Tier 2-3 cities
- 500+ collection points
- Technology-light operations

**Viettel Post**
- Viettel Group subsidiary
- Leverages telecom infrastructure
- 1,000+ transaction points
- Electronic customs services

### International Operators

**DHL Vietnam**
- International express leader
- 6 major facilities
- Time-definite services
- Pharma and healthcare focus

**FedEx/TNT**
- International freight and express
- Strong air freight capabilities
- Multinational client base

**Kerry Express**
- Thailand-based, regional network
- SME-focused services
- Cross-border expertise

### E-commerce Logistics

**Shopee Xpress (SPX)**
- Platform-owned fulfillment
- Integrated with Shopee ecosystem
- 200+ sorting centers
- Flash delivery pilots

**Lazada Logistics**

**J&T Express**

## Last-mile Delivery

### Urban Last-mile
- **Same-day delivery**: Available HCMC, Hanoi, Da Nang
- **2-hour delivery**: Limited products, central districts
- **Cost**: $0.80-2.00 per delivery (urban)
- **Attempts**: 1.5 average per successful delivery

### Rural Last-mile
- **Coverage**: 95% communes reachable
- **Time**: 3-7 days (vs 1-2 urban)
- **Cost**: $3-8 per delivery (rural)
- **Challenge**: Low order density, return logistics

### Delivery Models
1. **Hub-and-spoke**: Central sorting, radial distribution
2. **Micro-fulfillment**: Small urban warehouses
3. **Locker networks**: Pickup points (growing)
4. **Crowdsourced**: Independent drivers (GrabExpress, etc.)

## Warehousing Infrastructure

### Total Stock
- Modern warehousing: 3-4 million sqm (grade A/B)
- Industrial parks: Integrated logistics zones
- Cold storage: 1.2 million cubic meters (growing rapidly)

### Key Locations
- **HCMC Region**: 40% of modern warehouse stock
- **Hanoi Region**: 25% of modern warehouse stock
- **Da Nang**: Central region hub
- **Bac Ninh, Binh Duong**: Manufacturing adjacency

### Warehouse Types
- **General**: Multi-purpose facilities
- **Bonded**: Customs supervision, FTZ access
- **Cold chain**: Temperature-controlled (2-8Â°C, frozen)
- **E-commerce**: High throughput, robotics pilots

### Technology Adoption
- WMS (Warehouse Management Systems): 30-40% penetration
- Automation: Limited, picking optimization
- RFID: Tracking in select facilities
- Drones: Not operational (regulatory)

## Freight and Long-haul

### Road Transport
- **Fleet**: 800,000+ commercial vehicles
- **Container trucks**: 50,000+ units
- **North-South axis**: Primary corridor (1,800km)
- **Congestion**: HCMC port area, Hanoi ring roads

### Ports and Shipping
- **Container ports**: Cai Mep (deep water), HCMC cluster
- **Throughput**: 20+ million TEU annually
- **Feeder services**: Intra-Asia connectivity
- **Challenges**: Port congestion, customs delays

### Air Freight
- **Noi Bai (Hanoi)**: Northern hub
- **Tan Son Nhat (HCMC)**: Southern hub (capacity constrained)
- **Long Thanh Airport**: Under construction (2026+ operations)
- **Volumes**: Electronics, garments, seafood

### Rail Freight
- **Network**: 3,000+ km, meter and standard gauge
- **Volume**: Limited, 3% modal share
- **Potential**: China connectivity, bulk goods
- **Challenges**: Speed, reliability, last connection

## Supply Chain Characteristics

### Manufacturing Integration
- Just-in-time: Electronics, automotive
- Vendor managed inventory: Retail sector
- Cross-docking: FMCG distribution

### Import/Export Flows
- **Imports**: China, Korea, Japan, ASEAN
- **Exports**: US, EU, China, Japan
- **Processing trade**: Re-export manufacturing

### Cold Chain
- **Pharma**: Temperature-sensitive drugs
- **Fresh produce**: Fruits, vegetables, flowers
- **Seafood**: Export quality frozen
- **Dairy**: Growing domestic consumption

## Technology and Innovation

### Digital Platforms
- **Logistics matching**: Uber-style freight (LoG, Go2Joy)
- **Fleet management**: GPS tracking, route optimization
- **Warehouse exchanges**: Unused capacity platforms

### Automation
- **Sorting centers**: Automated in major hubs
- **AGVs**: Limited warehouse deployment
- **Delivery robots**: Pilots only (regulatory)

### Data Analytics
- **Demand forecasting**: Inventory optimization
- **Route optimization**: Fuel and time savings
- **Predictive maintenance**: Fleet management

## Costs and Economics

### Cost Structure (Typical 3PL)
- Labor: 35-40%
- Fuel/energy: 25-30%
- Depreciation: 15-20%
- Other: 15-20%

### Pricing Benchmarks
- **City courier**: $0.80-2.00 per kg
- **Inter-city (HCMC-Hanoi)**: $0.15-0.25 per kg
- **Warehousing**: $4-8 per sqm per month
- **Cold storage**: $8-15 per cubic meter

## Regulatory Environment

### Key Regulations
- **Road transport**: Driver hours, vehicle standards
- **Customs**: E-customs, bonded warehouse regulations
- **Safety**: Dangerous goods transport
- **Environment**: Euro emission standards (implementing)

### Foreign Investment
- **Logistics**: 100% foreign ownership allowed (WTO commitment)
- **Road transport**: Requires local partnership
- **Warehousing**: Fully open

### Infrastructure Investment
- **Expressways**: 1,900+ km operational, expanding
- **Deep-water ports**: Cai Mep-Thi Vai development
- **Logistics centers**: Government-planned zones

## Challenges

### Infrastructure Gaps
- **Last-mile**: Rural road quality
- **Port capacity**: Tan Son Nhat airport constraints
- **Connectivity**: Inter-provincial road links

### Cost Pressures
- **Fuel costs**: 25-30% of operating costs
- **Labor**: Rising wages, driver shortages
- **Land**: Urban warehouse costs increasing

### Efficiency Issues
- **Empty runs**: 35-40% of truck kilometers
- **Consolidation**: Fragmented market, scale challenges
- **Standardization**: Limited pallet/container standards

## Future Outlook

### 2025-2030 Developments
- **Long Thanh Airport**: Cargo capacity expansion
- **High-speed rail**: Potential freight services
- **Expressway network**: 5,000+ km target
- **Logistics costs**: Target 12-14% of GDP (from 16%)

### Technology Trends
- **Electric vehicles**: Fleet electrification pilots
- **Blockchain**: Supply chain traceability
- **IoT**: Real-time cargo monitoring
- **Autonomous**: Long-term potential
