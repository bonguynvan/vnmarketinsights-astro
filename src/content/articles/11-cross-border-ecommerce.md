# Cross-border E-commerce Vietnam

## Executive Summary

Cross-border e-commerce represents 15-20% of Vietnam's total e-commerce GMV, with Vietnamese consumers purchasing $3-4 billion annually from international sellers. The market is characterized by strong demand for foreign brands, price arbitrage, and evolving regulatory oversight.

## Market Size and Structure

### Import Volume
- **Cross-border GMV (2024)**: $3-4 billion
- **Share of e-commerce**: 15-20% of total
- **Annual growth**: 20-25%
- **Transactions**: 50+ million orders annually

### Transaction Characteristics
- **Average order value**: $60-80
- **Items per order**: 2-3 items
- **Category concentration**: Electronics, fashion, beauty
- **Shipping time**: 7-21 days standard

## Import Flows by Origin

### China (Dominant Source)
- **Market share**: 45-50% of cross-border GMV
- **Platforms**: Taobao, Tmall, 1688, AliExpress
- **Categories**: Electronics accessories, fashion, home goods
- **Price advantage**: 30-50% vs domestic
- **Daigou phenomenon**: Personal shoppers popular

### Korea
- **Market share**: 15-18%
- **Categories**: Beauty, fashion, baby products
- **Drivers**: K-culture influence, quality perception
- **Platforms**: Coupang, Gmarket, brand sites
- **Shipping**: Fast (3-7 days), reliable

### Japan
- **Market share**: 8-10%
- **Categories**: Cosmetics, home goods, supplements
- **Drivers**: Quality, authenticity trust
- **Platforms**: Amazon Japan, Rakuten
- **Price**: Premium positioning

### United States/Europe
- **Market share**: 12-15%
- **Categories**: Luxury, authentic brands, specialty items
- **Platforms**: Amazon, eBay, brand websites
- **Shipping**: Slow, expensive
- **Drivers**: Authenticity, unavailable locally

### Southeast Asia (Regional)
- **Market share**: 5-8%
- **Countries**: Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia
- **Categories**: Regional brands, specialty food
- **Platforms**: Shopee cross-border, Lazada Global

## Consumer Demographics

### Profile
- **Age**: 25-40 years old (70%)
- **Income**: Middle to upper-middle class
- **Location**: Urban concentrated (80%)
- **Education**: Higher education common
- **English proficiency**: Moderate to high

### Motivations
1. **Price**: 40% (lower prices overseas)
2. **Authenticity**: 30% (genuine products)
3. **Selection**: 20% (unavailable locally)
4. **Quality**: 10% (superior products)

### Shopping Behavior
- **Research intensive**: Compare prices, reviews
- **Platform familiarity**: Comfortable with foreign sites
- **Payment flexibility**: Multiple methods
- **Patience**: Accept longer shipping

## Platform Landscape

### International Platforms

#### Amazon
- **Vietnamese users**: 5+ million
- **Prime**: Limited availability
- **Shipping**: Long (10-20 days), expensive
- **Categories**: Electronics, books, specialty
- **Payment**: Cards, international wallets

#### AliExpress
- **Users**: 8+ million
- **Positioning**: Direct from China
- **Shipping**: ePacket, AliExpress Standard
- **Price**: Very competitive
- **Challenges**: Quality inconsistency

#### eBay
- **Niche usage**: Collectibles, used items
- **Vietnamese sellers**: Active (exports)
- **Payment**: PayPal required

### Regional Platforms

#### Shopee (Cross-border)
- **Global collection**: International sellers
- **Localized**: Vietnamese interface
- **Shipping**: Shopee logistics
- **Payment**: Local methods accepted

#### Lazada (LazGlobal)
- **Cross-border storefront**: Within app
- **Categories**: Electronics, fashion, beauty
- **Advantage**: Familiar interface
- **Shipping**: Lazada logistics

### Daigou Services
- **Definition**: Personal shopping agents
- **Operation**: Purchase on behalf, ship to Vietnam
- **Fee structure**: 10-20% commission
- **Popular for**: Taobao, complex purchases
- **Communication**: Zalo, Facebook groups

## Product Categories

### Electronics and Accessories
- **Share**: 30-35% of cross-border
- **Items**: Phone accessories, small gadgets, components
- **Origin**: Primarily China
- **Motivation**: Lower prices, variety

### Fashion and Apparel
- **Share**: 25-30%
- **Styles**: Korean fashion, international brands
- **Platforms**: Taobao, Korean sites
- **Returns**: Difficult, costly

### Beauty and Personal Care
- **Share**: 20-25%
- **Preference**: Korean, Japanese products
- **Authenticity**: Major concern
- **Regulation**: Cosmetic registration required

### Baby and Maternity
- **Share**: 8-10%
- **Drivers**: Safety concerns, quality
- **Origins**: Japan, Korea, Europe

### Home and Living

## Logistics and Delivery

### Shipping Methods
- **Postal (VNPost)**: Economical, slower
- **Courier (DHL, FedEx)**: Fast, expensive
- **ePacket**: China-specific, balanced
- **Sea freight**: Bulk orders, very slow
- **Cross-border rail**: Emerging (China)

### Delivery Times
- **China**: 7-14 days (standard)
- **Korea**: 5-10 days
- **Japan**: 10-15 days
- **US/EU**: 14-30 days
- **Express**: 3-7 days (premium cost)

### Tracking and Visibility
- **End-to-end tracking**: Standard expectation
- **Last-mile**: Handoff to local couriers
- **Notifications**: SMS, app updates
- **Issues**: Delays at customs common

## Payment Methods

### International Cards
- **Visa/Mastercard**: Primary method
- **Issuance**: Local banks, international banks
- **Limitations**: FX fees, fraud concerns
- **Usage**: 40-45% of payments

### PayPal
- **Adoption**: Moderate, growing
- **User base**: 2+ million accounts
- **Advantage**: Buyer protection
- **Limitation**: Limited local integration

### Alipay/WeChat Pay
- **Usage**: For Chinese platforms
- **Requirement**: Chinese bank account
- **Alternative**: Through daigou

### Local Methods (via platforms)
- **Shopee/Lazada**: E-wallets, COD
- **Cards**: Domestic card acceptance
- **Advantage**: Familiar payment flow

## Regulatory Framework

### Customs and Duties
- **De minimis threshold**: 1 million VND (~$40)
- **Below threshold**: No duty/tax
- **Above threshold**: VAT (10%) + import duty
- **Enforcement**: Increasingly strict

### Product Restrictions
- **Prohibited**: Weapons, drugs, counterfeit
- **Restricted**: Cosmetics (registration), food (safety)
- **Electronics**: Safety certification (MIC)
- **Medicine**: Strict import controls

### Registration Requirements
- **Cosmetics**: FDA Vietnam registration
- **Food**: Import license, safety certificates
- **Electronics**: Type approval (radio devices)
- **Documents**: Varies by category

### Consumer Protection
- **Limited**: Difficult to enforce across borders
- **Platform protection**: Depends on marketplace
- **Payment protection**: Card chargebacks (limited window)
- **Dispute resolution**: Challenging
