# Fintech Landscape Vietnam

## Executive Summary

Vietnam's fintech sector has experienced explosive growth, with transaction volumes increasing from $5 billion in 2018 to over $18 billion in 2024. The market is characterized by mobile-first adoption, QR code standardization (VietQR), and a rapidly expanding digital lending ecosystem.

## Market Size and Growth

### Transaction Volume
- 2024 total digital payment volume: $18-20 billion
- Annual growth rate: 30-40% (2020-2024)
- Mobile wallet transactions: 60% of volume
- Bank digital channels: 35% of volume
- Other fintech: 5% of volume

### User Adoption
- E-wallet users: 75+ million registered accounts
- Active e-wallet users: 45-50 million (monthly)
- Mobile banking users: 40+ million
- Digital lending borrowers: 8-10 million

## Payment Infrastructure

### National Payment Switch (NAPAS)
- Real-time interbank transfers (24/7)
- VietQR standard implementation
- 60+ banks connected
- Transaction processing: <3 seconds

### VietQR Standard
- Unified QR code format across banks and wallets
- Compatible with international QR standards (EMVCo)
- Static and dynamic QR support
- Zero-cost transfers between participating banks

### Payment Methods Breakdown

#### E-wallets
1. **MoMo** (M-Service)
   - 30+ million users
   - 250,000+ merchant acceptance points
   - Services: payments, transfers, investments, insurance
   - Agent network: 100,000+ points

2. **ZaloPay** (Zalo Group)
   - 15+ million users
   - Integrated with Zalo messaging (80M users)
   - Social payments (red envelopes, group transfers)
   - Mini-app ecosystem

3. **ViettelPay** (Viettel Group)
   - 12+ million users
   - Strong rural penetration
   - Leverages Viettel's telecom infrastructure
   - Micro-lending services

4. **ShopeePay** (Sea Group)
   - 10+ million users
   - E-commerce focused
   - Shopee ecosystem integration
   - Cross-border payment capabilities

5. **VNPay**
   - QR infrastructure provider
   - Banking app integrations
   - 40+ bank partnerships
   - Government payment services

#### Banking Apps
- Vietcombank Digital: 8+ million active users
- Techcombank Mobile: 6+ million active users
- Timo (VPBank): Digital-first banking
- Cake by VPBank: Neobank offering

## Digital Banking

### Traditional Bank Digitalization
- 95% of transactions now digital (major banks)
- Branch visits declined 60% (2019-2024)
- Video KYC implementation
- Biometric authentication adoption

### Neobanks
- **Timo**: 500,000+ customers, savings-focused
- **Cake by VPBank**: Lifestyle banking, cashback rewards
- **LiveBank by TPBank**: Video teller integration
- Foreign neobanks: Limited presence due to licensing

### Open Banking
- Pilot program launched 2023
- Account information sharing (with consent)
- Payment initiation services
- Standardized APIs by NAPAS

## Digital Lending

### Consumer Lending
- Market size: $3-4 billion (2024)
- Average loan size: $200-500
- Tenure: 3-12 months typical
- APR: 18-40% (regulated ceiling: 40%)

### Key Players
- **Home Credit Vietnam**: POS lending leader
- **FE Credit**: Consumer finance (VPBank subsidiary)
- **HD Saison**: Electronics, vehicle financing
- **Doctor Dong**: Salary advance, short-term loans

### Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)
- Market size: $500M+ (2024)
- Providers: Kredivo (Indonesia), Home Credit, local players
- Typical: 3-6 installments, 0% merchant-funded
- E-commerce integration: Shopee, Lazada, Tiki

### P2P Lending
- Regulatory framework pending
- Limited legal operations
- Informal P2P activity exists
- Crowdfunding: Under development

## Insurtech

### Digital Insurance Distribution
- Online premiums: 15% of total (growing from 5% in 2020)
- Embedded insurance: E-commerce, ride-hailing
- Micro-insurance: $1-5 policies (accident, health)

### Major Players
- **Bao Viet**: Traditional leader, digital push
- **Prudential Vietnam**: Digital agent tools
- **FWD**: Digital-first life insurance
- **Micro-insurance**: Momo, other wallet integrations

## Wealthtech

### Digital Investment Platforms
- Stock trading apps: Growth of retail investors
- **TCBS**: Techcombank securities app
- **SSI Mobile**: Leading brokerage platform
- **Funds**: Online mutual fund platforms emerging

### Crypto Assets
- Cryptocurrency trading: Technically restricted
- Underground activity persists
- Blockchain pilots: Government sanctioned
- NFT market: Limited, speculative

## Regulatory Framework

### State Bank of Vietnam (SBV)
- Primary regulator for payments and lending
- Licensing for payment services (e-wallets)
- Consumer credit regulations
- Anti-money laundering (AML) compliance

### Key Regulations
- **Circular 19/2016/TT-NHNN**: Payment services
- **Circular 39/2016/TT-NHNN**: E-money issuance
- **Circular 18/2019/TT-NHNN**: Consumer credit
- **Decree 163/2018/ND-CP**: Lending activities

### Licensing Requirements
- E-wallet license: Minimum capital 50 billion VND (~$2M)
- Payment intermediary: SBV approval required
- Foreign ownership: Caps in certain payment services

### Consumer Protection
- Transaction dispute resolution (NAPAS)
- Data privacy: Personal Data Protection Decree 2023
- Cooling-off period: 3-7 days for credit products
- Transparent fee disclosure requirements

## Cross-border Payments

### Remittances
- Inbound: $18-19 billion annually (diaspora)
- Digital channels: 30% of remittances
- Major corridors: US, Australia, Japan, Korea
- Fintech providers: Remitly, Wise, local banks

### Trade Finance
- Digital letters of credit adoption
- Blockchain pilots (Vietnam Trade Information Portal)
- SME financing gaps: $4-5 billion unmet demand

## Challenges

### Financial Inclusion
- 60% of adults have formal bank accounts
- Credit access: 25% of SMEs have bank loans
- Rural areas: Lower digital payment adoption

### Infrastructure
- Interoperability: Improving but gaps remain
- Real-time settlement: Available for domestic
- Cross-border: Slow, expensive for non-USD

### Trust and Literacy
- Digital fraud incidents: Rising concern
- Financial literacy: 45% of adults (low)
- Elderly population: Lower adoption rates

## Future Outlook

### 2025-2027 Projections
- Digital payment volume: $35-40 billion
- E-wallet users: 60+ million active
- Digital lending: $8-10 billion
- Neobank customers: 2+ million

### Emerging Trends
- AI for credit scoring
- Voice-based banking
- Super-app financial ecosystems
- Green fintech (carbon tracking, sustainable investing)
