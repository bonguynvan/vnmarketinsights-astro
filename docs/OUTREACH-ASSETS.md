## 1. First data-drop post (publish on site + LinkedIn/Medium)

**Title:** Vietnam in 2025, in 8 numbers

> Vietnam closed 2025 as one of Asia's fastest-growing economies. Here's the year in eight
> figures — all from official sources, free to cite.
>
> 1. **GDP grew 8.02%** to ~$514B — among Asia's fastest. *(GSO/NSO)*
> 2. **GDP per capita reached $5,026**, up ~$326 on the year. *(GSO/NSO)*
> 3. **Exports hit ~$475B (+17%)**; total trade topped **$930B (+18%)** with a **~$20B surplus**. *(GSO/NSO)*
> 4. **Disbursed FDI reached a record ~$27.6B (+9%)** — the highest in five years. *(GSO/MPI)*
> 5. **QR-code payment value jumped +159.6% YoY** (volume +66.7%) in the first 7 months. *(SBV)*
> 6. **E-commerce revenue hit ~$31B**, ~10% of retail. *(MoIT/VECOM)*
> 7. **The VN-Index closed +41%** near 1,784, with market cap ~$387B (~84% of GDP). *(HOSE)*
> 8. **79.8M Vietnamese are online** (~79% penetration); 76.2M on social. *(DataReportal)*
>
